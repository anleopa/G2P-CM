Tissue Expression

Description

Tissue or organ where the genes are turned on to synthetize a functional gene product

References

  • GTEX: https://commonfund.nih.gov/gtex

Attributes

Name Type Description
tissue_name String

Name of the tissue or organ where the gene is expressed
expression_value String

Measure used to determine the level of expression of a gene

Sources and Tools

GTEx Portal: Comprehensive public resource to study tissue-specific gene expression and regulation.
Human Protein Atlas: Swedish-based program initiated in 2003 with the aim to map all the human proteins in cells, tissues and organs using integration of various omics technologies, including antibody-based imaging, mass spectrometry-based proteomics, transcriptomics and systems biology.
NCBI Gene: Gene integrates information from a wide range of species. A record may include nomenclature, Reference Sequences (RefSeqs), maps, pathways, variations, phenotypes, and links to genome-, phenotype-, and locus-specific resources worldwide.
UniProt: Comprehensive, high-quality and freely accessible resource of protein sequence and functional information.
